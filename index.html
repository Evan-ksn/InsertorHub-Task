Retail shareholder FAQ

Answer the questions a retail investor is already asking.

Why is gross margin down?

Peak promotional trading supported sales but reduced gross margin. The next-half watch item is whether lower promotional intensity supports margin recovery.

Are stores working?

Early signals are positive: store conversion is 11.1%, stores mature over 12–18 months, and omni customers have 20% higher LTV.

Why invest in fulfilment now?

The National Fulfilment Centre should support scale, automation and efficiency, with payback forecast under four years.

What does iKOU add?

iKOU adds owned-brand economics and a multi-channel growth platform across retail, direct-to-consumer and wholesale.

What should I watch next half?

Gross margin recovery, store maturity, active customer growth, loyalty/app contribution, inventory control and NFC/ERP execution.
